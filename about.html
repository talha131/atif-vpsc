About

Vanguard Public School & College (VPSC) is dedicated to providing quality education from Pre-School to College. We foster academic excellence, holistic development, and critical thinking. Our modern facilities, diverse programs, and student-focused mentorship emphasize creativity, innovation, and lifelong learning. VPSC prepares students for 21st-century challenges and nurtures future leaders.

About Us

Vanguard Public School & College (VPSC) provides quality education from Pre-School to College. We foster academic excellence, holistic development, and critical thinking. Our modern facilities, diverse programs, and student-focused mentorship emphasize creativity, innovation, and lifelong learning.

  • Academic Excellence

    We ensure academic excellence through a rigorous curriculum and dedicated faculty.

  • Holistic Development

    Our programs support the social, emotional, and physical growth of students.

  • Innovative Learning

    We embrace innovative learning methodologies to foster critical and creative thinking.
